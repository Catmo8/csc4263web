OUR VISION

Since their debut in the mid 2000s, the popularity of commercial virtual reality games has skyrocketed. Our team is highly interested in the immersive and engaging nature of virtual and augmented reality environments which have proven to be so captivating for players. Because of this interest, we wanted to dedicate our time in this class to developing our own virtual reality game. Our goal is to create a unique game with equal focus on gameplay, storyline, and aesthetics that will provide users with a full and well-rounded gameplay experience that they will not find anywhere else in the virtual reality game market.
